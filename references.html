References

All sources cited throughout this website, in APA 7th edition format

All references are formatted in APA 7th edition. Academic sources are listed first, followed by web and organisational sources. In-text citations throughout this website use the author-date format (e.g., Chesney & Citron, 2019). All DOIs and URLs were verified at the time of submission.
