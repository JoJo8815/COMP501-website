Team Contract Summary

Our group signed a Team Contract at the start of the project setting out our agreed working norms, communication expectations, and meeting guidelines. Key commitments:

  • All members attend scheduled meetings or notify the group in advance if they cannot.
  • Work is distributed equitably and reviewed by at least one other member before submission.
  • Communication happens through our group chat, with replies expected within 24 hours on weekdays.
  • Disagreements are resolved through group discussion; the group leader has a casting vote if needed.
  • All members sign off on final submissions before they are uploaded to Canvas.
