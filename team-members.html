Team Contract Summary

Our group signed a Team Contract at the start of the project outlining our agreed code of conduct, communication norms, and meeting guidelines. Key commitments include:

  • All members will attend scheduled meetings or notify the group in advance if unable to attend.
  • Work will be distributed equitably and reviewed by at least one other member before submission.
  • Communication will happen primarily via our group chat, with a response expected within 24 hours.
  • Disagreements will be resolved through discussion; the group leader has a casting vote if needed.
  • All members sign off on final submissions before they are uploaded.
